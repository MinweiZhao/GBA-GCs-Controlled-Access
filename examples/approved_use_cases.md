# Example Uses

## Potentially Approved

- reproducibility audit of benchmark preprocessing
- non-commercial urban morphology research with aggregate reporting
- method comparison requiring AOI geometry under controlled conditions
- local validation of model outputs by urban studies researchers
- robustness testing of open reconstruction workflows

## Not Approved

- surveillance or geofencing
- law-enforcement operational targeting
- real-estate marketing, pricing, or ranking
- per-compound public lookup tools
- linkage to resident-level or household-level data
- redistribution of raw archives or extracted geometries
