# Controlled Release Policy

The full release contains AOI boundaries, community names, raw image chips, and provider-derived metadata. It is not appropriate for unrestricted public GitHub release.

Recommended sharing conditions:

- Non-commercial academic research only.
- No re-identification or linkage to resident-level data.
- No redistribution of raw files.
- No publication of precise coordinates, AOI polygons, names, or addresses.
- Aggregate reporting only unless a component is independently public and license-compatible.
- Deletion or return of files at project end if required by the DUA.
- Prompt reporting of suspected misuse, breach, or accidental exposure.

## Prohibited Uses

- surveillance, policing, geofencing, or operational targeting
- real-estate pricing, advertising, or commercial profiling
- ranking or scoring individual residential compounds
- linking with household, resident, phone, mobility, or transaction records
- publishing maps that reveal controlled per-compound identities or coordinates
- redistribution to third parties or public mirrors
